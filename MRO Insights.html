🚀 New Product & SKU Focus Spotlight — 2025

⚡ Dissolving Strips (Feb–May Push → Dec Revival)
Strips launched quietly in Jan, then peaked Feb–May with heavy sampling. Interest cratered Jun–Nov, then Dec saw a holiday revival. Customers loved the concept and convenience but consistently asked for more caffeine, better taste, and a hair growth strip version. University students were a standout audience for the affordable Energy Strips.
💪 Creatine Gummies (Sep Launch)
Launched in Aug, then surged in Sep — the strong sales of Creatine Gummies during the vitamin sale. Feedback was polarized: gym-goers loved the gummy format but consistently said the dosage was too low (3g vs the 5-10g they take in powder). Some found the texture gritty with a bitter aftertaste. Personal trainers questioned efficacy of low-dose creatine. Q4 performed strongly.
🐉 Dragon Fruit Melon Hair Growth (Aug Launch)
First appeared in Aug strong performance, then built steadily — by Nov, the strong sales of (the peak month). Customers were excited for a new flavor alternative to peach mango. Feedback was mostly positive on taste. Many asked for smaller 15oz bottles and travel/pouch sizes. By Dec, shoppers were actively requesting it, and some wished for the dragon fruit in a sugar-free gummy version too.
🌊 Sea Moss (Steady Riser)
Sea Moss products were available all year. Liquid Extract peaked in Aug strong performance, while Gummies sold steadily (~30-75 total/mo). The green apple gummy flavor was a crowd-pleaser — BAs compared it to "Jolly Rancher candy." Customers frequently asked for sea moss gel format. By 2026, the sea moss gel recall from competitors drove new interest to MRO's versions.

🛒 Most Requested Products & Formats — 2025

Sourced from 1,872 customer responses to "What items would customers like to see MRO make?" (field added Feb 2025) plus shopper comments and quotes captured by brand ambassadors throughout the year.
🥇 Capsule / Pill Form — 113 mentions
The single most requested format change. Customers across all demographics want vitamins in capsule/pill form as an alternative to liquid and gummies. Multivitamins are the #1 ask for capsule format. Key driver: some customers can't tolerate gummy texture, sugar content, or liquid taste.
"I wish she made vitamins in pill/capsule form" · "Capsule form for each vitamin always comes up" · "I don't do gummies so I wish they had calcium in liquid or capsule form"
🥈 Sugar-Free Options — 98 mentions
Persistent demand for sugar-free versions across the line — especially kids' gummies, probiotic gummies, and vitamin C gummies. Parents are the most vocal group. Some want sugar-free versions of products that only exist in organic (sugar-containing) form.
"I wish all her products were sugar-free" · "Sugar-free MV gummies for all genders and ages" · "I love the kids gummies but wish the probiotic one were also sugar-free"
🥉 Collagen Products — 59 mentions
Significant demand for collagen in multiple formats — gummies, powder, and liquid. Customers are aware MRO is vegan and some specifically ask for vegan collagen-boosting supplements. Others would buy non-vegan collagen from MRO if available.
"Collagen Gummies" · "Powder collagen" · "Collagen boosters, D-Ribose for collagen production"
4. Immunity / Cold & Flu — 50 mentions
Customers want dedicated cold/flu and allergy relief products. Currently addressed partially through Elderberry, Oil of Oregano, and Vitamin C, but shoppers want a clear "immunity" SKU — potentially a kids version of the 7-in-1 Immunity Support Liposomal.
"People keep mentioning cold and allergy medicines would be great" · "A mom wants the 7-in-1 Immunity Support but for kids" · "Immunity strips"
5. Protein / Muscle Building — 48 mentions
Demand for protein powder (sugar-free chocolate was specifically requested), muscle-building supplements, and higher-dose creatine. The creatine gummy launch in Sep partially addressed this but dosage complaints persist (3g vs desired 5-10g).
"Many were shopping for protein" · "Muscle building supplements for men and women" · "Everyone is looking for more protein"

🚀 SKU Focus — 2026 YTD

⚡ Energy Strips — Dominant in 2026 (avg YTD)
Energy Strips surged to #4 overall SKU (from #7 in 2025). Customers love the convenience — especially for office workers and conference calls. Recurring asks: more caffeine, new flavors beyond strawberry, and caffeine-free option. Mood & Cognition and Sleep strips saw minimal traction (under 20 total each) — energy is the clear winner of the strip format.
🐉 Dragon Fruit Melon — Surged to #2 SKU (avg YTD)
Was not in 2025's top 10, now the #2 product by demand. Customers are actively seeking it out. The sea moss gel recall from competitors also drove interest in MRO's sea moss products as replacements.
💪 Creatine Gummies — Strong Momentum (avg YTD)
Broke into the top 5 as a new product. Gym-goers love the gummy format but dosage complaints persist. Demand is there — shoppers consistently say they wish it had 5g+ per serving instead of 3g. The convenience factor keeps driving interest despite the dosage gap.
